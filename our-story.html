We offer free yoga classes all summer long. Classes are outside in June, July and August at both Main Street & at Kits Beach. We also bring yoga to you! We offer our yoga services to business and institutions who want yoga in their space.

Our mission is to foster a new type of yoga studio. Nonprofit, outdoor yoga classes coupled with yoga mat recycling. All in beautiful Vancouver BC. Support our mission by volunteering, donating or sponsoring here.
