Come join us for free yoga!

Be a part of Vancouver's largest outdoor yoga classes

Why free Classes? Because we are truly captivated by our students. Everyone is welcome. Many individuals have shared their stories with us when they come to our yoga classes. Whether it is because they are strapped for cash, going through a tough divorce, or can't afford physiotherapy and are living out of their car, we believe access to fitness classes needs to be accessible to everyone no matter their income bracket.
