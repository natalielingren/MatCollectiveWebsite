Overview of Project with MatCollective

For this project, we chose to focus on a an already existing non-profit business called MatCollective. We found that their current website has a lack of information architecture, and has problematic and confusing navigation. After meeting with the founders of the company, we refocused our angle on the landing page to reflect their wishes for more upcoming events and company happenings. After discussing the desired flow through the website, we focused on creating an easier to move through navigation system of tabs. The immense amount of information was more easily divided this way. We plan on taking this project further and will be meeting with the founders again to discuss possible integration of our designs into their actual website.
