Volunteer Position

Vancouver BC
We are always looking for volunteers to help us out. Whether you want to learn more about the yoga industry, gain experience with a start up or work on creating green products, we are here to help you out! Various volunteer positions are available, subject to your interests!
  • Sample of volunteer activities:
  • taking and/or editing photos
  • writing and/or creating content for social media, our blog, our website
  • R&D for our green products
  • assisting in organizing and tidying our studio space
  • painting furniture
  • event planning and preparation
  • rolling, labeling and cleaning yoga mats
  • other activities not listed here

Hours: 1 hour per shift minimum (flexible hours) ​Location: Merakos Fieldhouse @ 95 E 32nd Ave, Vancouver BC Apply By: no deadline Start Date​: no set date Compensation: none, option to work towards a free yoga mat ​To apply, email a brief description of why you are interested in volunteering with us to info@matcollective.com, stating the job title in the subject line. Thank you to all applicants, we will contact you shortly after receiving your application!
