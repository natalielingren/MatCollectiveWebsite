Yoga Mat Rentals

Rent a Recycled Yoga Mat!

How it works

  1. Individuals, yoga studios and other facilities donate unwanted mats to the collective. We are always accepting donations at our studio, and can organize pick up for large donations. We accept mats in any condition.
  2. Mats are sorted and washed with an organic cleanser. They are then labelled either for recycling (exchanged for a few volunteer hours or a small donation) or are upcycled (turned into new products.
  3. To drop off mats, volunteer or purchase products please coordinate a time to visit our studio.
